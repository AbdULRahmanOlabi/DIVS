SMS & LMS

SMS

School management system is an information management system for educational institutions to manage student data. It helps teachers get information about students faster and easier and reduces their workload. School management systems provide skills such as student registration, class documentation, grades, student analytical scores, and other assessment items. In addition, school management systems are used to plan student's curricula, record their presence, and manage student's needs in the school

Login To SMS

LMS

It is a software application for management, documentation, tracking, reporting, automation, and delivery of educational courses, training programs, materials, or learning and development programs. The concept of a learning management system has emerged directly from e-learning. Learning management systems make up the largest portion of the learning systems market.
